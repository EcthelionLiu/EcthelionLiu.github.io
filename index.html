I'm Jiaxi Liu, a master student at Department of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiaotong University majoring in Computer Science.

I previously worked as a research intern at Microsoft, Beijing. During my undergraduate study, I'm honored to work with Prof. Mingsheng Long, Prof. Yue Gao, and Prof. Xibin Zhao at Tsinghua University. In addition, I am fortunate to be a research visitor at University of California, San Diego, working with Prof. Zhuowen Tu last summer.

My research interests lie in LLM , Computer Vision, AI4Science, and generative AI. My research goal is to help machines see and understand this world through scientific and interpretable ways.
